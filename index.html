Three steps, same as picking up any online order — except this one saves food and money.

Collect & track impact

    Sell your surplus food

    Anyone can sell — restaurants, supermarkets, bakeries, sweet shops, caterers, tiffin services, or an individual running a home kitchen — as long as you hold a valid Indian FSSAI food license. Licensing is what makes someone eligible, not the size of the business.

    Find this on your FSSAI certificate or at foscos.fssai.gov.in

    Registration format-checks your FSSAI number. A team member verifies it against FoSCoS before you can publish listings.
